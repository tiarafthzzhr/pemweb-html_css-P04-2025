William Henry Gates III, famously known as Bill Gates, is an American business magnate, software developer, investor, author, and philanthropist. He is best known as the co-founder of Microsoft Corporation.

Beyond his technological achievements, Bill Gates is also a prominent philanthropist. Alongside his ex-wife Melinda French Gates, he co-founded the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, the world's largest private charitable foundation, which works to enhance healthcare and reduce extreme poverty globally.
